Your Trust is Our Primary Warp and Weft.

At LoomLore Academy, we treat your personal data with the same precision we apply to a complex tapestry. We don't just teach weaving; we safeguard the threads of information you share with us. How do we ensure your privacy stays pristine?

Core Privacy Commitment

Our commitment to your privacy isn't just a legal checkbox. It's a fundamental part of how we operate in the digital textile space. Whether you're enrolling in a high-fidelity weaving course or buying bespoke yarns, your data stays within our looms. We use encrypted payment processing via industry leaders, meaning we never store your credit card digits on our own servers. It's safe, it's efficient, and it's essential.

We've spent 12 years building a community based on craft and trust. Your email isn't a commodity to us; it's a direct line for education. We'll never sell your contact info to third-party marketing firms because that's just poor craftsmanship.

The Cookie Jar

Why do we use cookies? Mostly to remember your loom preferences and cart items. These tiny files help us understand which textile history modules are trending so we can create better content for you. You're always in control of your browser settings.

Data Access

Want to see what we have on file? Just ask. We believe in total transparency.

Communications

Subscribe for art tips, unsubscribe whenever you like. No strings attached.

Third Party Partners

We only partner with tools that respect artisan values. This includes our hosting providers and educational platforms. Each partner is vetted for compliance with global standards like GDPR and CCPA to ensure our New York-based academy meets world-class requirements.
