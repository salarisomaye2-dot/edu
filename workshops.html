Master the Loom in the Heart of Ridgewood

Ready to turn thread into something tactile and timeless? Our NYC studio is more than just a classroom; it's a living laboratory for fiber arts enthusiasts. We don't just teach weaving. We foster a community where historical techniques meet modern sustainability.

Won't you join us at the bench?

View Availability

Professional Equipment

At 937 Onderdonk Avenue, our studio is equipped with high-end Schacht Spindle and Leclerc floor looms. Whether you're interested in basic 4-shaft structures or exploring complex patterns on a 12-shaft system, we've got the hardware to support your vision.

Expert Guidance

Meet our lead instructor, Muelas Accashian. With 14 years of experience in hand-weaving and a deep knowledge of sustainable dye practices, Muelas ensures every student finds their rhythm.

Weekend Intensive

Friday prep, Saturday warping, Sunday weaving. A complete cycle.

Sustainable Practices

We're committed to the planet. You'll work exclusively with reclaimed fibers and ethically sourced organic wool. Waste not, weave well.

Take-Home Art

You'll leave with your own hand-woven textile piece.

Reserve Your Loom

Our hands-on sessions at LoomLore Academy are limited to 6 participants to ensure individual attention. No experience is required—just curiosity.
