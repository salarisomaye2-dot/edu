Regulatory Framework & Service Agreement

Before we weave together, it's vital to establish the threads of our professional relationship. We've designed these terms to protect both the heritage of the craft and the curiosity of our students.

Are you ready to commit to the standards of LoomLore Academy?

Essential

General Terms of Service

By accessing our courses or New York studios, you're agreeing to these guidelines. We believe in transparency. If you don't agree with the minutiae here, we'll unfortunately have to restrict your access to our looms and digital archives.

Our curriculum is dynamic. We reserve the right to modify course content to reflect current sustainable textile practices without prior notification. It's how we stay at the cutting edge for our 1,200+ global alumni.

Cancellations

Life happens. But weaving requires preparation. Cancellations made within 48 hours of a workshop incur a 50% fee. Why? Because we've already warped the loom for you. No-shows are ineligible for refunds.

Intellectual Property

The drafting patterns and techniques shared in our courses are proprietary to LoomLore Academy. You're encouraged to sell the fabrics you create, but sharing our core instructional PDFs is a no-go. Respect the craft.

Studio Safety

Working with heavy machinery like floor looms involves inherent risks. Students must follow all safety protocols provided by our instructors. We aren't liable for minor nicks or stains acquired during the creative process. Wear your apron!
